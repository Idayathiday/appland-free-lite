Comprehensive Logistics & Business Management Software

Our platform offers a suite of powerful modules designed to optimize logistics workflows and enhance business operations. Whether you're managing international shipping or employee time tracking, we've got you covered.

1. End-to-End Logistics Software


Our logistics module covers every touchpoint in the supply chain:

Customs Clearance & Forwarding: Automate documentation, declarations, and approvals with built-in compliance tools.

Flight Forwarding: Manage air freight bookings, schedules, and cargo status in real-time.

ICD & CFS Operations: Efficiently coordinate container movements and yard operations with complete visibility.

3PL Integration: Seamlessly manage third-party logistics with transparent tracking and reporting.

Warehouse Management: Real-time inventory control, barcode/RFID integration, and stock movement tracking.

Transport Management: Route optimization, vehicle tracking, driver performance monitoring.

Courier Services: Smart dispatching, parcel tracking, delivery proof, and mobile support for field staff.

2. Attendance Management System

Track employee attendance, shift schedules, and leave requests with biometric or mobile check-ins. Perfect for field teams, warehouses, or corporate offices.

Features:
Biometric, RFID, or GPS-based check-in.

Shift planning and overtime tracking.

Integration with payroll systems.

3. Contract Management System

Organize and control contracts with vendors, customers, or partners.

Features:
Contract creation, versioning, and e-signatures.

Renewal reminders and approval workflows.

Secure document storage with audit trails.

4. Asset Management System

Monitor and maintain your company’s assets—whether it’s vehicles, equipment, or IT hardware.
